Returns & Exchanges

Luxury Grade

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your accessories, you may return or exchange them within 30 days of receiving your order.

Eligibility for Returns & Exchanges

To qualify for a return or exchange, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Unused & Unworn – Items must be in their original condition with tags attached
  • Original Packaging – Products should be returned in their original packaging
  • Proof of Purchase – A valid order number or receipt is required

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Personalized or monogrammed accessories
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Damaged or altered products (unless the damage was caused during shipping)

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

1

Contact Us

Email us at luxurygradeofficial@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return/exchange.

2

Return Authorization

We'll provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and instructions.

3

Ship Your Item

Securely pack the item and ship it to the provided return address.

4

Processing

Once received, we'll inspect the item and process your refund or exchange within 5-7 business days.

Refund & Exchange Options

Refund
